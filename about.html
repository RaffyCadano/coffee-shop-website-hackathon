Welcome to Mr. Coffee

At Mr. Coffee, we believe that every cup tells a story. As a family-owned and operated coffee shop based in Winston-Salem, North Carolina, we are passionate about crafting the perfect coffee experience for our community.

Our mission is simple: to bring people together over exceptional coffee, warm hospitality, and an inviting atmosphere. We take pride in serving high-quality, freshly brewed coffee, along with a selection of handcrafted beverages and delicious treats.

Friendship

More than just a coffee shop, Mr. Coffee is a place where friendships are made, creativity flows, and every guest feels at home. We are dedicated to supporting our local community and making Winston-Salem a recognized destination for coffee lovers and beyond.

Whether you're here for your morning boost, an afternoon break, or a cozy space to unwind, we invite you to experience the warmth and quality that define Mr. Coffee.
